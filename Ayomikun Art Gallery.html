Ayomikun Ojoade: The Prolific Maestro of Realism

Hailing from Lagos, Nigeria, Ayomikun Ojoade is a rising star in contemporary art. Known for his striking realism in charcoal and oil, his work speaks volumes, capturing emotion and detail with mastery.

A Yaba College of Technology and Universal Studios of Art alumnus, Ayomikun’s work has earned celebrity commissions from Temi Otedola, Falz, Darey Art Alade, and Helen Paul. Beyond portraits, his contributions to an exhibition on the war in Ukraine highlight his role as a global storyteller.

Achievements

  • The Adventure – Joint Exhibition (02/2020)
  • Hansford and Sons Virtual Showcase – London (06/2020)
  • ARTS FROM THE MOTHER LAND – Thames Art Center, New York (08/2022)
