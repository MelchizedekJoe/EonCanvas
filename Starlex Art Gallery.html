About the Artist

Where Culture
Meets Craft

Simi Adey, known as Starlex, is a Fashion Designer and Textile Artist whose work fuses timeless elegance with the richness of African heritage. Each piece is a meditation on identity, culture, and beauty.

Trained through formal education, hands-on experience, and mentorship, Simi brings a sophisticated eye and deep cultural literacy to every creation — from bespoke garments to large-scale textile installations.

She is currently exploring bold textile techniques and sustainable materials for a new portrait series that pushes the boundary between fashion and fine art.

Education: Trained in fashion design through formal education, hands-on experience, and industry mentorship.
